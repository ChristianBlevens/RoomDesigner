# Scale

The scale slider adjusts the size of all furniture and the meter stick in the room proportionally. This is useful because the AI-extracted 3D geometry may not perfectly match real-world scale.

## Opening the Panel

Tap the **scale button** (resize icon) in the controls bar. The panel opens below it. Tap again or press the X to close.

## How It Works

- The slider controls a room-wide scale multiplier (default 1.0)
- All furniture and the meter stick scale together — individual pieces maintain their relative sizes
- Each piece of furniture has a base scale calculated from its dimensions, which is then multiplied by the room scale

## Calibration Workflow

1. Place a real meter stick (or object with known size) in the room before photographing
2. After the room is created, use the **meter stick** tool to place the virtual reference
3. Position the virtual meter stick next to the real one in the photo
4. Adjust the scale slider until the virtual meter stick matches the real object's size
5. All furniture is now correctly scaled to the room

## Tips

- Scale is saved per room, so different rooms can have different scale values
- The scale panel and lighting panel are mutually exclusive — opening one closes the other
