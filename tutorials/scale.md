# Scale

The scale slider adjusts the size of all furniture in the room proportionally. This is useful because the AI-extracted 3D geometry may not perfectly match real-world scale.

## Opening the Panel

Tap the **scale button** (resize icon) in the controls bar. The panel opens below it. Tap again or press the X to close.

## How It Works

- The slider controls a room-wide scale multiplier (default 1.0)
- All furniture in the room scales together — individual pieces maintain their relative sizes
- Each piece of furniture has a base scale calculated from its dimensions, which is then multiplied by the room scale

## Tips

- Use the **meter stick** to calibrate — place it next to a known-size object in the photo (like a door frame or counter) and adjust scale until the meter stick matches
- Scale is saved per room, so different rooms can have different scale values
- The scale panel and lighting panel are mutually exclusive — opening one closes the other
