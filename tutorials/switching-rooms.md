# Switching Rooms

## How to Switch

Tap any **room tab** at the bottom of the screen to switch to that room. The current room is automatically saved before switching.

## What Happens

- The current room's furniture, lighting, scale, and meter stick are saved
- The new room loads with all its saved state restored
- Undo history is cleared when switching rooms
- The before/after toggle state persists across room switches within the same house
