# Deleting a Room

## How to Delete

1. **Long-press or right-click** on a room tab to open the room action menu
2. Tap **Delete** to remove the room

## What Happens

- The room and all its data (furniture layout, lighting, mesh) are permanently deleted
- If this was the only room, the house closes and you return to the calendar
- If other rooms remain, the app switches to the first available room
- This action cannot be undone
