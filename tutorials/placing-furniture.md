# Placing Furniture

## How to Place

1. Tap on any empty surface in the room (floor, wall, or ceiling)
2. The furniture database modal opens
3. Browse or search for the item you want — items with a "3D" badge have placeable models
4. Tap a furniture card to place it at the position you tapped

## Surface Alignment

- Furniture automatically detects which surface you tapped and aligns accordingly
- On **floors**: furniture stands upright in its natural orientation
- On **walls**: furniture lays flat against the wall
- On **ceilings**: furniture hangs from above

## Availability

- Each furniture card shows how many are available based on your house's date range
- Items that are fully booked by overlapping houses show zero availability
- You can still place items at zero availability, but it indicates a conflict
