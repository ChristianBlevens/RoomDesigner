# Sharing a House

Share a house with clients or team members via a public link. The share page shows staged room images, a delivery manifest, and download options.

## How to Share

1. Tap the **House** button in the controls bar
2. Tap **Share House**
3. A share link is generated — copy it, open it in a new tab, or revoke it

## Share Page

Anyone with the link can view:

- **Room cards** — each room with its staged image (or empty background if no image has been generated yet)
- **Before/after slider** — when both background and staged images exist, drag the slider to compare
- **Furniture list** — per-room breakdown showing name, category, and condition
- **Delivery manifest** — aggregated inventory grouped by category, with counts, storage location, and condition
- **Download** — download individual room images or all at once

## Generating Staged Images

If you are signed in as the house owner and visit the share page:

1. Each room shows a **Generate Image** button
2. Tapping it captures a screenshot of the room with all placed furniture and lighting
3. You are asked whether to **enhance with AI** — this blends furniture naturally into the room lighting
4. The generated image is saved and immediately visible on the share page

## Revoking a Share Link

1. Tap the **House** button → **Share House**
2. Tap **Revoke Link** — the share URL will stop working immediately
3. Sharing again generates a new link

## Notes

- Share links are per-house — each house gets its own unique URL
- Revoking a link does not delete generated images; sharing again creates a new link
- Generated images are stored on the server and persist across sessions
- The share page uses the same visual theme as the main app
