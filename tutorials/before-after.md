# Before / After

The before/after toggle lets you compare the empty room photograph against the staged version with furniture.

## Usage

Tap the **before/after button** (split rectangle icon) in the controls bar. It highlights when active.

- **Active** — all furniture and shadows are hidden, showing just the room photo
- **Inactive** — furniture and shadows are visible as normal

## Notes

- The toggle is session-scoped: it resets when you load a new house but persists when switching between rooms in the same house
- The meter stick is not affected by before/after — it stays visible independently
- This is purely a visual toggle and does not affect saved data
