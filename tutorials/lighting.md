# Lighting

The lighting panel lets you control the scene's directional light to match the room's natural lighting.

## Opening the Panel

Tap the **lighting button** (sun icon) in the controls bar. The panel opens below it. Tap again or press the X to close.

## Controls

- **Intensity** — slider from 0 to 3. Controls how bright the light is. Default is 1.0
- **Temperature** — slider from warm (2000K, orange) to cool (10000K, blue). Default is 5500K (neutral white)
- **Shadow** — slider from 0 to 1. Controls how dark the shadows are. At 0, shadows are invisible. At 1, shadows are fully opaque. Default is 0.5
- **Direction** — tap "Set Direction" then press and drag on the scene to aim the light. A gizmo appears showing the light source and target. You can also drag the gizmo endpoints to adjust

## Tips

- Match the light direction to window positions or visible light sources in the photo
- Warm temperature works well for cozy interiors, cool for modern or office spaces
- The light casts shadows from furniture onto the room surfaces
- Lower the shadow intensity if shadows look too harsh against the room photo
- Cancel a direction change by pressing "Cancel" to restore previous settings
