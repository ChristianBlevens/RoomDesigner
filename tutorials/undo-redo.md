# Undo / Redo

Undo and redo let you reverse or re-apply furniture actions.

## Supported Actions

- **Place** — undo removes the placed furniture, redo places it back
- **Move** — undo returns furniture to its previous position
- **Rotate** — undo restores the previous rotation
- **Delete** — undo brings back the deleted furniture

## Usage

- Tap the **undo** button (curved left arrow) to reverse the last action
- Tap the **redo** button (curved right arrow) to re-apply it
- Keyboard shortcuts: **Ctrl+Z** for undo, **Ctrl+Shift+Z** for redo

## Notes

- The undo history is cleared when you switch rooms or close a house
- The meter stick is not tracked by undo — deleting it is permanent
