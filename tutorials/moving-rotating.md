# Moving & Rotating Furniture

## Selecting

Tap on placed furniture to select it. A popup menu appears with three options:

- **Move** (arrows icon) — shows a 3D translate gizmo
- **Rotate** (circular arrow icon) — shows a 3D rotation gizmo
- **Delete** (trash icon) — removes the furniture from the room

## Dragging

Press and drag furniture to slide it along its surface. The furniture stays on the same surface type it was placed on — floor furniture stays on the floor, wall furniture stays on the wall.

## Transform Gizmo

After selecting move or rotate mode:

- **Move mode** — drag the colored arrows to move along an axis, or drag the squares to move along a plane
- **Rotate mode** — drag the colored rings to rotate around an axis

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- **G** — enter move mode on selected furniture
- **R** — enter rotate mode on selected furniture
- **Delete / Backspace** — delete selected furniture
- **Escape** — deselect furniture

## Tips

- Tap selected furniture again to reopen the popup menu
- Furniture snaps to the surface as you drag, following the room's 3D geometry
- The app smooths surface normals for stable alignment on uneven mesh areas
