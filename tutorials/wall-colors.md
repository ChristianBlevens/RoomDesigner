# Wall Colors

Change the wall color of a room using AI. The app generates a new version of your room photo with the walls repainted in your chosen color.

## Choosing a Color

1. Tap the **paint button** (paint roller icon) in the controls bar
2. Pick a color:
   - Tap a **preset swatch** to load it into the color picker
   - Or choose any color with the **color picker** and type a name
3. Tap **Apply** to generate the new wall color

Generation takes a few seconds. A spinner shows in the gallery while processing.

## Switching Between Variants

The gallery at the bottom of the panel shows all generated variants plus the original. Tap any card to switch the room background to that version.

- The **Original** card restores the unmodified room photo
- Active variant is highlighted with a blue border
- Each variant card shows a colored bar matching the wall color

## Deleting a Variant

Hover over (or long-press on mobile) a variant card to reveal the delete button. Tap it to remove that variant. If the active variant is deleted, the room reverts to the original photo.

## Notes

- Wall color variants are saved with the room and persist across sessions
- Variants are deleted automatically when the room, house, or org is deleted
- The wall color panel closes when switching to lighting, scale, or layouts panels
