# Calendar

The calendar is the landing page after signing in. It shows all your houses organized by their scheduled dates.

## Navigation

- Use the **arrow buttons** to move between months
- Houses appear as colored bars spanning their date range
- Tap a **house bar** to load that house
- Tap a **date** to see all houses scheduled on that day (useful when bars are too small to tap on mobile)

## Creating a House

Tap the **"+" button** on any date to create a new house starting on that date. You'll be prompted to enter a name and end date.

## Tools & Feedback

Tap the **"Tools"** button at the bottom of the calendar to access:

- **Furniture Segmenter** — open the segmentation tool in a new tab
- **Send Feedback** — submit feedback or suggestions, and view your previous submissions with status updates and admin responses
- **Tutorials** — browse help guides for all features (also accessible via the **?** button in the calendar footer)

## Color Coding

Each house gets a unique color for easy identification on the calendar grid.
