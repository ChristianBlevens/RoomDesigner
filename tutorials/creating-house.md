# Creating a House

## From the Calendar

1. Tap the **"+" button** on any date in the calendar
2. Enter a **name** for the house
3. Set the **start date** and **end date** for the staging period
4. Tap **Create**

## House Editor

The house editor modal validates that:

- The name is not empty
- The end date is on or after the start date

## After Creation

The new house appears on the calendar. Tap it to load it, then add rooms with the "+" tab.
